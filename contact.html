Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a holiday?

You can book on our website or call us. We will help you pick the best holiday for you.

Can I pay in parts?

Yes! You can pay a deposit first and then pay the rest later before you go.

What if I need to cancel?

If you cancel more than 30 days before you get full money back. Less than 30 days you get half back.

Is travel insurance included?

No, but we can help you get insurance. We recommend you always get travel insurance.
